About Us
Suzi was born and raised in Brazil and moved to the US when she was 12, while John was born and raised in New York. It was definitely not love at first sight when they first met at UVA. Though they were friends, they were the meanest of friends. Playful or not, their interactions were nothing but hateful comments and death glares to each other that entire first year of school. Second year came around and through a series of group hangouts and unexpected events, such as John selflessly,generously, kind-heartedly helping Suzi move her couch into her apartment, they started to see the positives in one another. They were “not bad” and “alright” as they would describe one another. Through God’s grace, Suzi and John dated all throughout college and even after graduation where they both reside in Mclean, Virginia. Currently, John is a consultant at Deloitte while Suzi is a medical student at Georgetown University. Though John is busy with his job, he still makes time to support Suzi in her studies by cooking for her and by listening to her complain about the million exams she has to take.

Groomsmen:
Nissi Kim (Best Man)
Loud, obnoxious, arrogant, and annoying were my first impressions of Nissi. I ended up becoming apartment mates with Nissi for two years and through God’s grace, I realized how rash my judgement was. I learned how passionate this guy is about his many hobbies and interests. I learned how he isn’t afraid to voice his opinions and point out your faults without being single-minded. Most importantly, I learned how good of a friend he is. Even after graduation, he goes out of his way to keep touch and takes time out his schedule to hang out. No matter what part of the world he is in, we have always managed to keep in touch and I’m truly grateful for that. He’s still loud though. But I don’t mind as much.

Aaron Lim
I don’t have many NY friends anymore even though I was born and raised there. Thankfully, Aaron is one of the few that kept me as a close friend. We don’t meet up often, but whenever we do, we are able to pick it back up from where we left off. I like to think our countless rounds of playing pool had something to do with that. Aaron is one of the smartest and the most trustworthy guys that I know. Adversity doesn’t stop this guy from getting the job done and doing what is right. That is the trait that I admire most about him. It saddens me to think that our limited number of hangouts will probably wither away to none. But I know for sure that he will do big things in Cali and I can’t wait for what God has in store for him.

James Kang
James is one of the funniest guys that I know. He never fails to make me laugh with his lack of athleticism and his hideousness. I like how our conversations are laden with quotes and references from our favorite TV shows, Simpsons & ParksandRec. It’s unnatural how much this guy remembers these shows by heart. James is energetic, hard-working, and very genuine. His goal of becoming a missionary might take him overseas, but I hope we can always keep in touch. Also, I will deny all of this in front of James so he doesn’t get the satisfaction. I will say instead he’s still unathletic and hideous.
