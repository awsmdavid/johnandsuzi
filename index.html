About Us
Suzi was born and raised in Brazil and moved to the US when she was 12, while John was born and raised in New York. It was definitely not love at first sight when they first met at UVA. Though they were friends, they were the meanest of friends. Playful or not, their interactions were nothing but hateful comments and death glares to each other that entire first year of school. Second year came around and through a series of group hangouts and unexpected events, such as John selflessly,generously, kind-heartedly helping Suzi move her couch into her apartment, they started to see the positives in one another. They were “not bad” and “alright” as they would describe one another. Through God’s grace, Suzi and John dated all throughout college and even after graduation where they both reside in Mclean, Virginia. Currently, John is a consultant at Deloitte while Suzi is a medical student at Georgetown University. Though John is busy with his job, he still makes time to support Suzi in her studies by cooking for her and by listening to her complain about the million exams she has to take.
